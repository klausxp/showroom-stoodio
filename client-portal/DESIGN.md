# Design System: Functional Minimalist Portal

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
**Aesthetic:** Functional Minimalism.
**Mood:** Professional, clean, and trustworthy. Focus on clarity and ease of use.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Slate Navy (#1A2B3C):** Primary identity and sidebar.
- **Cool Grey (#EDF2F7):** Background.
- **Vibrant Blue (#3182CE):** Primary actions and notifications.
- **Success Green (#38A169):** Project status indicators.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Headers:** Inter (Sans-serif) - Bold, professional.
- **Interface Text:** Inter (Sans-serif) - Regular, optimized for data density.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Tables/Grids:** Clean, no borders. Zebra striping with whisper-soft colors.
* **Badges:** Rounded corners, pastel backgrounds for status.
* **Cards:** Subtly elevated with soft shadows to separate modules.

## 5. Layout Principles
- **Sidebar Navigation:** Persistent for easy access.
- **Data-First:** Prioritize important metrics and dates.
